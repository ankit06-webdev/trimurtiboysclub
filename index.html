About Us

Tri Murti Boys Club, established in 1998, is a vibrant community dedicated to fostering cultural and spiritual traditions. Rooted in devotion and unity, the club takes pride in organizing grand celebrations of Saraswati Puja, Ganesh Puja, and Maha Shivratri every year.

With unwavering enthusiasm, the members come together to arrange beautifully adorned pandals, devotional rituals, and community gatherings that bring people of all ages closer to their heritage. Through these events, TriMurti Boys Club not only preserves age-old traditions but also strengthens the bonds of brotherhood, faith, and social harmony among its members and the local community.

- Tri Murti Boys Club
